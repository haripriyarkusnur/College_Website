World's Largest University

Jain University, officially JAIN (Deemed-to-be University), is a private deemed-to-be-university located in Bangalore,Davangere,Hubli,Belgavi,India. Originating from Sri Bhagawan Mahaveer Jain College, it was conferred the deemed-to-be-university status in 2009.
